*Interactive visualization of whest's symmetry detection algorithm.*

**You will learn:**
- How the bipartite graph, incidence matrix, and sigma-loop work visually
- How symmetry is detected step-by-step for any einsum expression
- How FLOP savings are computed from the detected symmetry group

Choose a preset example or define your own einsum expression with custom operand symmetries to walk through the explorer’s four-act narrative:

1. **Set up the contraction** — inspect a preset or build a custom expression, then compare the expected symmetry with the analyzed result
2. **Expose the structure** — view the bipartite graph and incidence matrix that encode the contraction
3. **Prove the symmetry** — follow the σ-loop and generator construction that recover the detected group
4. **Price the savings** — connect orbit structure to multiplication and accumulation savings
